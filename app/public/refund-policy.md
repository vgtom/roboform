# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date:** 2 April 2026

At VinForms ("we", "our", or "us"), we aim to provide a reliable and high-quality service. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which payments, cancellations, and refunds are handled.

By purchasing, subscribing, or buying a one-time offer (such as a **Lifetime Deal**), you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. No Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our service, which includes AI-powered processing and usage-based infrastructure costs, all purchases—including **subscriptions** and **one-time / Lifetime Deal** payments—are **non-refundable** once completed, except where Section 5 (Exceptional Refunds) applies.

When you use VinForms, computing resources and third-party AI services are consumed immediately, making it infeasible to reverse costs after usage.

We strongly encourage users to evaluate our starter plan before upgrading to higher-tier plans.

---

## 2. Subscription Plans and Billing

VinForms offers subscription-based pricing.

By subscribing, you agree that:

* You will be billed on a recurring basis (monthly or as specified)
* Your subscription will automatically renew unless canceled
* You are responsible for managing your subscription and cancellation

---

## 3. One-Time Purchases (Lifetime Deal)

We may offer **one-time** products (for example, a “Lifetime Deal” or similar prepaid access) for a single upfront payment processed through our payment providers.

For these purchases:

* There is **no recurring subscription** or automatic renewal tied to the one-time fee itself. If you also maintain a separate subscription, that subscription is governed by Section 2 and Section 4.
* The same **non-refund** rules in Section 1 apply: completed one-time payments are not refundable except as described in Section 5 (Exceptional Refunds).
* Plan limits (such as AI usage caps) and the scope of access are those described at checkout and on our pricing pages at the time of purchase.
* **Cancellation** in the sense of “end of billing period” (Section 4) does not apply to a standalone one-time purchase; there is no future billing cycle to cancel.

---

## 4. Cancellation Policy (Subscriptions)

You may cancel your subscription at any time.

Upon cancellation:

* Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
* You will not be charged for future billing cycles
* No partial or prorated refunds will be issued

---

## 5. Exceptional Refunds

Refunds may be granted at our sole discretion in limited circumstances, including:

* **Duplicate payments** due to billing errors
* **Technical issues** where the Service was not accessible or failed to function as intended, and the issue was not resolved within a reasonable timeframe
* **Accidental charges**, if reported promptly and no significant usage has occurred

To request a refund under these conditions, you must contact us within **7 days of the transaction**.

---

## 6. Abuse and Misuse

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in cases where:

* The Service has been significantly used or consumed
* There is evidence of abuse, misuse, or violation of our Terms of Service
* Requests are made in bad faith or to exploit the system

---

## 7. Payment Processing

Payments for VinForms may be processed by third-party providers such as Lemon Squeezy, which may act as the Merchant of Record.

As a result:

* Refund processing times may depend on the payment provider
* Any approved refunds will be issued back to the original payment method

---

## 8. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without contacting us first:

* We reserve the right to suspend or terminate your account
* You may lose access to the Service immediately

We encourage users to contact us directly to resolve any billing issues.
